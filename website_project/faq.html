Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rental requirements?
Drivers must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid driver's license and a major credit card.


How can I make a reservation?
Reservations can be made only online by clicking the "Reserve" button and fill out the form.


What is included in the rental rate?
The rental rate includes the cost of the vehicle, basic insurance, and unlimited mileage.


What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and in some cases, cash.


What is your fuel policy?
The vehicle must be returned with the same amount of fuel as when it was rented. Otherwise, a refueling fee will apply.
